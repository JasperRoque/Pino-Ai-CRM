# Setting Up Your Pino Ai CRM Account

This guide will walk you through the process of setting up your Pino Ai CRM account and configuring it for your business needs.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit [Pino Ai CRM](https://www.pino-ai.com)
2. Click on "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
3. Enter your business information:
   - Business name
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Industry type

## Initial Configuration

### Step 1: Profile Setup
1. Upload your company logo
2. Set your business timezone
3. Configure business hours
4. Add team members

### Step 2: Customize Your Workspace
1. Set up custom fields
2. Configure lead stages
3. Create email templates
4. Set up automation rules

### Step 3: Import Data
1. Prepare your contact list
2. Map custom fields
3. Import contacts
4. Verify imported data

## Best Practices

- Use a business email for your account
- Set strong passwords
- Add team members early
- Configure notifications
- Set up backup options
