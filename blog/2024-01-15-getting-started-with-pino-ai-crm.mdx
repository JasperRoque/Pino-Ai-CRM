## Quick Start Guide

### 1. Sign Up for Your Account

Getting started with Pino Ai CRM is quick and easy:

1. Visit [www.pino-ai.com/pino-ai-crm](https://www.pino-ai.com/pino-ai-crm)
2. Click on "Start Free Trial"
3. Enter your business information
4. Verify your email
5. Log in to your new CRM dashboard

### 2. Set Up Your Business Profile

Take a few minutes to customize your CRM:

- Add your business logo
- Set your business hours
- Configure notification preferences
- Invite team members

### 3. Import Your Contacts

Bring your existing customer data into Pino Ai CRM:

- Import from CSV files
- Sync with your email contacts
- Connect with your existing tools
- Manual entry for new contacts

### 4. Configure Your Sales Pipeline

Set up your sales process:

- Define your sales stages
- Create custom fields
- Set up automation rules
- Configure email templates

## Best Practices for Success

1. **Start Small**: Begin with the essential features and gradually expand
2. **Train Your Team**: Ensure everyone understands how to use the system
3. **Use Templates**: Save time with pre-made email and document templates
4. **Track Everything**: Use the reporting tools to monitor your progress
5. **Regular Updates**: Keep your customer information current
